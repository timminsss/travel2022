Over 2 months we travelled a total of 8 flights, 5 buses, 4 boats and a load of taxis from Scotland to Australia.

Have a look at our itinery below.

Kuala Lumpur

We arrived in KL, and went sightseeing. We also swam in our rooftop pool and tried the local food.

We also did a day trip to Batu Caves (while v v v hungover).

🚌

Cameron Highlands

We went to Cameron Highlands and hiked one day - and got rained on A LOT!

I also ate a lot of strawberry icepoles.

🚌

Penang

We stayed in Georgetown which was very pretty - lots of street art and good food!

We also did a day trip to the national park and climbed Penang Hill.

🚌 -- 🛳

Langkawi

We went to Langkawi!

We went to a lot of beaches and Jeff tried out his moped skills so we could scoot round the island.

✈️ -- ✈️

Kota Kinabalu

We flew to KK from Langkawi.

We chilled by the pool and ate some Borneo food at the markets.

🚖

Kota Belud

We went to Kota Belud (Big Fin Divers) for some diving!

Spend a long weekend chilling by the beach and did a try dive! We even saw some nemos 🐠

🚖 -- ✈️

Sepilok

Sepilok is home to the Orangutan sanctuary 🦧

We saw quite a lot of orangutans and some sunbears too!

🚌

Kinabatangan River

We went to Kinabatangan River to see all the Borneo animals. We saw wild animals each time we went out on the boat - elephants, organutans, alligators, birds, and loads more!

🚌 -- ✈️

Ubud

Time was up in Malaysia, so we flew to Indonesia!

Ubud, Bali was the first stop where we stayed for a week and explored! 🚖 -- 🛳
